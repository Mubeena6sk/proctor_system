GUIDELINES


    Candidates are requested to take the test honestly, ethically, and should follow all the instructions.

  1. Candidate should be seated in front of the desktop or Laptop, facing the screen and webcam throughout the entire duration of the test.
  2. Candidate should be seated in a way that there is enough light to capture his/her face clearly.
  3. Candidate should be in formals or smart casuals while taking the test.
  4. No one else should be present in the surroundings other than the candidate during the test.
  5. Candidate CANNOT open any other application window during the test and if anybody is caught doing so, it will be considered as a violation.
  6. Candidate must keep in mind that their images and videos will be recorded and supervised in near real time by AI as well as a Human proctor. Therefore, it is strictly advised that the candidate does not leave the seat during the test. Such behavior will be immediately recorded as a violation.
  7. No bio breaks are permitted during the test.
  8. Candidate is not allowed to use a calculator during the test. A plain paper and a pencil are allowed for rough work.
  9. Candidate CANNOT talk or speak during the test, or use any device like phones or tablets for anything. If the candidate disturbs the position of the camera, the test will be auto submitted and will not be accessible and available to the candidate thereafter.
